Pizza Salami Recipe

This delicious Salami pizza is a great easy recipe that everyone will love.

Itâ€™s topped with Italian salami, banana peppers, red onion, and lots of cheese and is a definite winner!

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • marinara sauce
  • shredded mozzarella cheese and fresh mozzarella pearls
  • freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Italian salami
  • red onion
  • peppers
  • fresh basil or fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit, or an as hot as it will go. Place a pizza stone in the oven to let the stone heat. Prepare the pizza dough, whether homemade or from the store, so it is raised, and ready to roll out into a pizza.
  2. Sprinkle a pizza paddle (you can use metal or wooden) lightly with corn meal, and roll out the pizza dough into a large circle about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick (depending on your preferences).
  3. Spread the prepared pizza sauce over the top of the crust. Then evenly sprinkle it with mozzarella cheese. Add salami slices evenly over the cheese.
  4. Then spread peppers and red onion over the top of the salami if desired. Sprinkle grated parmigiano and fresh mozzarella balls over the top.
  5. Carefully transfer the pizza to the pizza stone and bake for 10-14 minutes, or until the cheese is evenly melted. (You can also bake it in a pizza oven until golden, we love ours)
  6. Garnish the pizza with parmesan, and fresh parsley or basil, and slice it up and enjoy hot.
